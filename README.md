# Supermart-Grocery-Sales-Analysis

<img width="423" height="228" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8ee1c75b-a31d-4edf-847c-0531e345f495" />


This project analyzes grocery sales data to identify revenue and profit drivers across categories, regions, and time.  
The focus is on **business-oriented insights** rather than just descriptive statistics.

---

##  Objective
The objective of this project is to:
- Analyze sales and profit performance
- Identify high and low-performing categories
- Study the impact of discounts on profitability
- Understand regional sales behavior
- Support data-driven retail decisions

---

##  Dataset Description
The dataset includes:
- Order details
- Product categories and sub-categories
- Sales, profit, and discount information
- Region and state details
- Order dates

Each row represents a retail transaction.

---

##  Key Focus Areas
- Sales vs profit comparison  
- Category and sub-category performance  
- Monthly and seasonal sales trends  
- Discount impact on profit margins  
- Region-wise sales analysis  

---

##  Data Cleaning & Preparation
Steps performed include:
- Removing missing and duplicate records  
- Converting date columns to datetime format  
- Creating year and month features  
- Standardizing numerical fields  

---

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Key visualizations include:
- Monthly sales and profit trends  
- Sales by category and sub-category  
- Top profit-generating products  
- Region-wise sales performance  
- Discount vs profit analysis  

---

##  Key Insights
- High sales volume does not always lead to high profit  
- Excessive discounts reduce profitability  
- A few sub-categories contribute most of the profit  
- Regional performance varies significantly  

---

##  Business Use & Impact
This analysis helps:
- Optimize inventory planning  
- Improve pricing and discount strategies  
- Focus on profit-driven categories  
- Design region-specific retail strategies  

---

##  Tools & Technologies Used
- Python  
- Pandas & NumPy  
- Matplotlib & Seaborn  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

##  Conclusion
The Supermart Grocery Sales Analysis highlights the importance of profit-focused decision-making. By combining sales, discount, and regional insights, the project demonstrates how data analytics can significantly improve retail performance.
